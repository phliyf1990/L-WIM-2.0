# Golden Silk Nanmu Style Guide

## 5-Dimensional Imagery Space (V-Space)

### 1. Variety (单一性 - 多样性)
- **Low**: Straight, uniform fibrous structure. Minimal figure.
- **High**: Holographic, complex, multi-layered "water-ripple" (Shuibo) patterns.

### 2. Gorgeousness (简约 - 华丽)
- **Low**: Subtle amber tones, matte finish, understated luxury.
- **High**: High-gold chatoyancy, liquid gold appearance, intense shimmer.

### 3. Dynamism (静态 - 动感)
- **Low**: Depth and stability. Pattern feels locked.
- **High**: Visual movement as light shifts. Shimmer flows across the surface.

### 4. Intensity (柔和 - 强烈)
- **Low**: Diffused warmth, gentle transitions.
- **High**: Sharp specular highlights, extreme contrast between shadow and gold.

### 5. Interest (乏味 - 有趣)
- **Low**: Traditional, predictable grain.
- **High**: Unique burl-like features (Huying), organic complexity, "interesting" anomalies.
